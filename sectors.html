Technology & IT

1,250+ jobs available

What They Do:

Technology professionals create, maintain, and improve digital systems that power our modern world.

Entry Level (0-2 years):
  • Junior Developer: Write basic code, fix bugs, learn frameworks
  • Data Analyst: Create reports, analyze trends, use Excel/SQL
  • IT Support: Help users, troubleshoot issues, maintain systems
Experienced (3-5 years):
  • Software Engineer: Design applications, lead projects, mentor juniors
  • Data Scientist: Build ML models, find insights, predict trends
  • DevOps Engineer: Automate deployments, manage cloud infrastructure
Senior Level (5+ years):
  • Tech Lead: Architect solutions, make technical decisions, guide teams
  • Principal Engineer: Define technology strategy, solve complex problems
  • AI/ML Engineer: Develop intelligent systems, research new algorithms
